# BPNL-Risk-Scoring-Analytics-India-
A comprehensive Data Analytics & Business Intelligence project that analyzes customer behavior, credit utilization, repayment trends, default risk, and competitor performance in the Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) ecosystem in India.
This project combines SQL, Python, Statistical Analysis, Machine Learning, and Power BI to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights and interactive dashboards for risk assessment and decision-making.

Project Objectives
Analyze customer demographics and borrowing behavior.
Identify factors influencing default risk and repayment performance.
Evaluate loan distribution and credit utilization patterns.
Compare leading BNPL companies based on lending performance.
Develop interactive Power BI dashboards for business intelligence.
Generate strategic recommendations for risk management and portfolio growth.

Tools & Technologies
SQL (DuckDB) – Data extraction, joins, aggregations, and business analysis.
Python – Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn for EDA and visualization.
Statistical Analysis – Descriptive Statistics, Gaussian Distribution, Correlation Matrix, T-Test, and Z-Test.
Machine Learning – Linear Regression using Scikit-learn for baseline model evaluation.
Power BI – Interactive dashboards with DAX measures and business KPIs.

Insights
Gen Z is the largest BNPL customer segment with the highest lending activity.
Higher credit utilization is strongly associated with increased default risk.
UPI users demonstrate better repayment performance than other payment methods.
Slice leads the market in total loan volume, while Simpl maintains stronger portfolio quality.
Customer behavior is highly non-linear, making Linear Regression unsuitable for accurate risk prediction.
